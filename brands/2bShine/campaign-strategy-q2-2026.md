## 3. TIKTOK AFFILIATE STRATEGY

### Sistem Kerja 100–300 KOL

**Tier Structure:**

| Tier     | Followers | Jumlah KOL | Komisi | Budget/KOL            |
|---       |---        |---         |---     |---                    |
| Nano     | 1K–10K    | 150–200    | 10–15% | Product only          |
| Micro    | 10K–100K  | 50–100     | 12–18% | Product + Rp 50–200K  |
| Mid-tier | 100K–500K | 5–10       | 15–20% | Product + Rp 500K–2jt |

**Alur Rekrutmen → Scaling:**

```
1. RECRUIT (Minggu 1-2)
   ↓ Cari via TikTok search: "skincare review" "skincare lokal"
   ↓ DM template: produk gratis + komisi 15% untuk 1 video
   ↓ Target: 100 KOL onboard bulan 1

2. BRIEF (Sebelum kirim produk)
   ↓ Brief 1 halaman: do's & don'ts, hook suggestion, hashtag
   ↓ Minta konten dalam 7 hari setelah terima produk
   ↓ Approval optional (jika ada klaim berlebihan)

3. CONTENT (Minggu 3-4)
   ↓ KOL posting konten
   ↓ Gunakan link affiliate TikTok Shop
   ↓ Track via kode unik per KOL

4. TRACKING (Ongoing)
   ↓ Monitor GMV per KOL setiap minggu
   ↓ Bayar komisi H+7 setelah konfirmasi pesanan
   ↓ Screenshot performa → kirim ke KOL (motivasi)

5. SCALING (Bulan 2-3)
   ↓ KOL dengan GMV > Rp 1jt → naikkan komisi 5%
   ↓ Beri exclusive deal: "Brand Ambassador 2bShine"
   ↓ Prioritaskan konten mereka untuk boost (TikTok Ads)
```

### Hook Konten High Converting

1. **"POV: Kulit kusam padahal rajin skincare"**
   → Relatable problem, banyak yang relate, CTR tinggi

2. **"Dermatologist bilang ini bahan aktif paling worth it di harga segini"**
   → Authority + value, edukatif, viral potential tinggi

3. **"Saya challenge pakai 2bShine 30 hari tanpa skincare lain"**
   → Progress video, bikin orang penasaran, completion rate tinggi

4. **"Beli skincare Rp 30rb vs Rp 300rb, bedanya apa sih?"**
   → Perbandingan, engaging, positioning value product

5. **"POV: Temen nanya pakai skincare apa sampe glowing gini"**
   → Aspirational, lifestyle fit, natural endorsement

### Sistem Komisi & Insentif

```
Komisi Base: 10–15% per transaksi via link affiliate
Bonus Milestone:
- GMV Rp 5jt/bulan → +5% bonus + exclusive gift
- GMV Rp 10jt/bulan → Brand Ambassador title + Rp 500K cash
- 3 bulan konsisten → Kontrak resmi (endorsement fee)

Non-cash insentif:
- Full PR package gratis setiap bulan
- Early access produk baru
- Feature di IG 2bShine (social proof untuk KOL)
- Mentoring skincare content dari brand
```

### Cara Konten Affiliate Bisa Viral + Selling

```
✅ Format: Talking head + close-up skin texture (ASMR skincare)
✅ Sound: Trending audio (update tiap minggu dari brief)
✅ Caption: "Link di bio / TikTok Shop" — jelas
✅ Timing: Upload sore 16.00–20.00 WIB
✅ Engagement bait: "Comment 'GLOW' buat aku kirimin info-nya"
✅ Authenticity: Real skin (jerawat, pori) — jangan over-edit
✅ CTA: Spesifik, clear — "Klik link TikTok Shop sekarang, stok terbatas"

❌ Jangan: Klaim berlebihan, before-after manipulatif, terlalu promotional
```

## 7. GROWTH HACK & SCALING STRATEGY

### Scale Winning Ads (Meta)

```
Step 1: Testing Phase (Minggu 1-2)
→ Jalankan 5-8 creative berbeda dengan budget kecil (Rp 50K/hari each)
→ Ukur CTR, CPC, dan conversion setiap 3 hari

Step 2: Identify Winners (Minggu 3)
→ Winning criteria: CTR > 2%, ROAS > 3x, CPA < Rp 20.000
→ Pause semua yang tidak memenuhi criteria

Step 3: Scale (Bulan 2)
→ Naikkan budget winning ads 20–30% per hari (jangan langsung 2x)
→ Duplicate ke audience baru (lookalike 3-5%)
→ Buat variasi creative dari winning angle
→ Expand ke placement (IG Stories, Facebook Feed, Audience Network)

Rule of thumb:
"Jika ROAS masih positif setelah scale 3 hari → scale lagi"
"Jika ROAS turun → stop scale, evaluasi creative"
```

### Scale Affiliate TikTok

```
Step 1: Identifikasi Top Performers (Bulan 1)
→ KOL dengan GMV > Rp 2jt/bulan = TOP TIER
→ KOL dengan konten views > 50K = CONTENT WINNER

Step 2: Double Down (Bulan 2)
→ Top GMV KOL: Boost konten mereka via TikTok Ads (Rp 50-100K/boost)
→ Content Winner: Minta konten series (3-5 video, same angle)
→ Naikkan komisi 5% untuk top performers

Step 3: Viral Loop (Bulan 3)
→ Ambil konten affiliate yang viral → jadikan iklan Meta & TikTok Ads
→ Buat "affiliate challenge" — siapa GMV tertinggi dapat cash bonus
→ Feature top KOL di IG Story 2bShine → mereka repost → loop awareness

Multiplier effect:
1 konten viral 500K views → 
500 klik ke Shopee → 
15 order (3% CR) → 
Rp 1.335.000 revenue dari 1 video gratis
```
